Functions

shift_left

Shift the contents of the given array left, i.e. towards negative indices, and replace the empty positions with the contents of replace. The amount to be shifted left is determined by replace.len().

shift_left_fill

Like shift_left, but shifts by a given amount and fills outside with the value set.

shift_right

Similar to shift_left, but in the opposite direction. The contents of replace are placed in the beginning of array.

shift_right_fill

Like shift_right, but shifts by a given amount and fills outside with the value set.
